# ЁЯМН EcoSphere - ESG Management Platform

> Odoo Hackathon 2026 Project

## ЁЯУМ Overview

EcoSphere is an intelligent ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) Management Platform developed for the Odoo Hackathon 2026.

The platform enables organizations to measure, monitor, and improve their ESG performance by integrating sustainability metrics, employee participation, governance compliance, and AI-powered insights into a unified dashboard.

---

## ЁЯЪА Problem Statement

Organizations struggle to manage ESG data because:

- Environmental data is scattered across multiple systems.
- CSR activities and employee participation are tracked manually.
- Governance compliance is difficult to monitor.
- ESG reports require significant manual effort.
- Employees lack motivation to participate in sustainability initiatives.

These challenges result in poor visibility, delayed reporting, and inefficient decision-making.

---

## ЁЯТб Our Solution

EcoSphere provides a centralized ESG Management Platform that:

- Calculates carbon emissions automatically.
- Tracks CSR activities and employee participation.
- Monitors governance policies and compliance.
- Encourages sustainability through gamification.
- Generates real-time ESG reports.
- Provides AI-powered sustainability recommendations.
